# Privacy Policy for GrowingDays

**Last Updated:** 19 July 2026

Welcome to GrowingDays ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your sensitive health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our menstrual and cycle tracking application (the "App").

By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve our services. **Crucially, your sensitive health and personal data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.**

### A. Information You Provide to Us
When you use the App, you may voluntarily provide the following information, which is stored **locally on your device**:
*   **Profile Information:** Your name and email address (used for personalization).
*   **Cycle and Period Data:** Start and end dates of your periods, average cycle length, and average period duration.
*   **Daily Health Logs:** Flow intensity, mood, pain/cramp levels, basal body temperature, and other symptoms you choose to log.

### B. Information Collected Automatically
To ensure the App runs smoothly and to improve user experience, we use third-party tools (Google Firebase) to collect technical data. This data **does not** include your health or personal profile information:
*   **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
*   **App Usage Data:** Screen views, feature interactions, and app performance metrics (via Firebase Analytics).
*   **Crash Data:** Stack traces and error logs when the App crashes or encounters a non-fatal error (via Firebase Crashlytics).
*   **Push Notification Tokens:** Generated to deliver local reminders and app updates (via Firebase Cloud Messaging).

### C. App Configuration Data
We use Firebase Realtime Database solely to fetch remote application configurations (such as force-update requirements or feature flags). No personal or health data is stored in or retrieved from this database.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
*   **To Provide Core Functionality:** To track your menstrual cycle, predict future periods, ovulation, and fertile windows.
*   **To Send Reminders:** To schedule and deliver local notifications for period predictions and symptom logging reminders.
*   **To Improve the App:** To analyze app performance, identify and fix bugs, and understand how users interact with the App.
*   **To Personalize Your Experience:** To display your name and tailor the App's insights to your specific cycle data.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

*   **Local-First Architecture:** Your sensitive health data (periods, symptoms, profile) and personal data (name, email) are stored exclusively in a local database (SQLDelight) on your physical device. 
*   **No Cloud Sync for Health Data:** We do not have a backend server that collects, stores, or processes your menstrual cycle or health data. 
*   **Device Security:** Because your data is stored locally, it is protected by your device's physical security and operating system protections. If you lose your device, we cannot retrieve your data.
*   **Data Deletion:** You can completely erase all your personal and health data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data from your device's settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services provided by Google. These services have their own privacy policies:

*   **Firebase Analytics:** Collects anonymous usage data to help us understand how the App is used.
*   **Firebase Crashlytics:** Collects crash reports and device information to help us fix bugs.
*   **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM):** Used to manage push notification tokens.
*   **Firebase Realtime Database:** Used strictly for fetching app configuration (e.g., update prompts).

You can review Google's privacy policy here: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 5. Permissions

To function properly, the App requests the following permissions on your device:
*   **Post Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`):** Required to send you reminders about your upcoming period or to log your daily symptoms. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings, but you will no longer receive reminders.

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Because your data is stored locally, you have full control over it:

*   **Right of Access:** You can view all the data we store by opening the App and navigating to your profile, settings, and calendar.
*   **Right to Rectification:** You can edit or update your cycle data, symptoms, and profile information directly within the App.
*   **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):** You can delete all your data by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's storage/cache in your device settings.
*   **Right to Restrict Processing:** You can restrict data collection by disabling Analytics in your device settings or revoking notification permissions.
*   **Right to Data Portability:** You can export your data (if the export feature is enabled in the App) to a readable format.
*   **Right to Object:** You can object to the collection of technical/crash data by disabling internet access for the App in your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions about your data, please contact us at the email provided below.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
