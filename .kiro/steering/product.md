# Product

This is a personal developer blog and portfolio site for Shahid Raza Ansari, a software engineer focused on Android development and backend engineering.

The site serves two purposes:
- **Portfolio**: Showcases skills, tech stack, and contact info on the homepage
- **Blog**: Technical articles covering Android, backend, ML/AI, and software engineering topics

The target audience is other developers. Content is technical in nature, written in English, and aimed at sharing engineering knowledge and experiences.

Live at: https://www.shahidraza.me
